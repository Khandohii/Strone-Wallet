Frequently Asked Questions
What is RFID?

RFID is an acronym that stands for radio-frequency identification. The tech encodes digital data into RFID tags and smart labels and readers capture that data via radio waves. Unless you have an RFID-blocking wallet like Strone™, this data is very easy to steal!

Will my cards get demagnetized?

Not at all! Besides protecting them from hackers, Strone™ does not affect the cards in it.

Can I remove the rubber band? I don’t carry cash with me.

Sure! If you don’t use cash, you can always remove the band, making Strone™ even more compact.

Is it easily scratched?

Between the high-strength carbon fiber and military-grade aluminum alloy, Strone™ is scratch-resistant, and will look like new after months of everyday use.

Do I have to assemble the wallet?

No! Strone™ comes already pre-assembled and ready for use. All you have to do is prepare to look much cooler than you looked before!
